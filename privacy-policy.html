ARCH.OPT Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

ARCH.OPT is a multi-objective building design optimization tool developed by Fuad Baba at The British University in Dubai (BUiD).

Data Collection

ARCH.OPT does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or user information. All computations and optimization processes are performed locally on your device.

Internet Access

ARCH.OPT uses an internet connection solely for license verification purposes. No personal data is transmitted during this process.

Third-Party Services

ARCH.OPT does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Storage

Any files or results generated by ARCH.OPT are saved locally on your device only. We have no access to these files.
