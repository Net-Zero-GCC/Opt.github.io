FAQ

Common questions

Can I try ARCHOPT before buying?

The installer is free to download, so you can confirm it installs and launches on your machine. Running optimizations requires an activated license key.

How is the license key delivered?

After your PayPal payment is confirmed, your license key is sent to the email address associated with your PayPal account — within 24 hours, usually much sooner.

Can I move my license to a new computer?

Each key activates on one machine. If you replace your computer, contact support through the purchase page and the license can be reset for activation on the new machine.

What happens after one year?

The license is valid for one year from activation. After expiry, the software prompts for a new key — renewing is the same one-time purchase, with no automatic billing.

Does ARCHOPT need an internet connection?

Only briefly: once for activation, and occasionally to re-verify the license. All simulations and optimizations run entirely on your own machine — your models never leave your computer.

Do I need to know how to code?

No. ARCHOPT is a desktop application with a full graphical interface. You define variables, ranges, and objectives in the GUI; the EnergyPlus runs, surrogate training, and NSGA-III optimization are fully automated.
